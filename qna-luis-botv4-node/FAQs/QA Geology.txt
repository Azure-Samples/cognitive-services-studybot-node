Question: What is magnitude?
Answer: Magnitude is the strength of an earthquake.

Question: What is magma?
Answer: Magma is molten rock that triggers earthquakes and creates volcanoes as it rises within the Earth.

Question: What is a metamorphic rock?
Answer: A metamorphic rock is a rock formed when another rock is changed by extreme heat or pressure.

Question: What is an era?
Answer: An era is a subdivision of the geologic time scale.

Question: What is a period?
Answer: A period is a subdivision of an era.

Question: What is an epoch?
Answer: An epoch is a subdivision of a period.
